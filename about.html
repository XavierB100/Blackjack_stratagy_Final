Game Rules & Settings

Standard Rules

  • Dealer stands on soft 17
  • Double down on any two cards
  • Double after split allowed
  • Split up to 3 times (4 hands)
  • Split Aces receive only one card
  • Blackjack pays 3:2
  • Insurance available
  • No surrender (can be enabled)

Customizable Options

  • Number of decks (1, 2, 4, 6, or 8)
  • Basic strategy hints on/off
  • Card counting practice mode
  • Betting amounts and limits
  • Game speed settings
  • Statistics tracking preferences
